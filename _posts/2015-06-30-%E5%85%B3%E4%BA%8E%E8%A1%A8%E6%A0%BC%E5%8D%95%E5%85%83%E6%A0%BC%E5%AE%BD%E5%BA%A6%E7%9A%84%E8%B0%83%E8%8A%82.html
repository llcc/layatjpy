By watching the video made by John Kitchin1, I realize a great handy trick of org-mode, which is shorten the column and justified.

Shorten the column for readability with <n> (n is a number), left justify using <l>

item description
short short description
long long description to describe the item

以上的数值和位置符号可以成功输入到latex和html中，但宽度还暂未能实现。
