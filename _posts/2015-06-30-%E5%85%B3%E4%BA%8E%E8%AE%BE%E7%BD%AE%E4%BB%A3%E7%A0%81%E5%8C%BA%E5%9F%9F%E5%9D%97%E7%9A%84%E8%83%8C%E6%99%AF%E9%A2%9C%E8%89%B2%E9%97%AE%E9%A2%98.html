--- title: "关于设置代码区域块的背景颜色问题" date: 2015-06-30 layout: post tags: Org ---

为了对比突出代码块与普通文本的区别，设置不同的底色是一个比较可行的方法。在.emacs文件中添加以下代码即可以上功能。或者在customize-org中查找org-block，然后选择自己喜欢的样式即可。org-mode总能给人惊喜！

{% highlight lisp linenos=table %} (setq org-src-fontify-natively t) (defface org-block-begin-line '((t (:underline "#A7A6AA" :foreground "#008ED1" :background "#EAEAFF"))) "Face used for the line delimiting the begin of source blocks.") (defface org-block-background '((t (:background "#FFFFEA"))) "Face used for the source block background.") (defface org-block-end-line '((t (:overline "#A7A6AA" :foreground "#008ED1" :background "#EAEAFF"))) "Face used for the line delimiting the end of source blocks.") {% endhighlight %}
