--- title: "Setup different face for org source block" date: 2015-06-30 layout: post tags: Org ---

Sometimes when your org files is jammed with org-babel blocks, it may be difficult to distinguish normal text and blocks. For this situation, setting different face is a good idea. Below cold can implement this kind of functions.

{% highlight c1 %} (setq org-src-fontify-natively t) (defface org-block-begin-line '((t (:underline "#A7A6AA" :foreground "#008ED1" :background "#EAEAFF"))) "Face used for the line delimiting the begin of source blocks.") (defface org-block-background '((t (:background "#FFFFEA"))) "Face used for the source block background.") (defface org-block-end-line '((t (:overline "#A7A6AA" :foreground "#008ED1" :background "#EAEAFF"))) "Face used for the line delimiting the end of source blocks.") {% endhighlight %}
